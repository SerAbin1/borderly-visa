Our Mission

A Better Alternative for Immigration Support

Borderly Visa was founded by Simon Biju and Anshif Rahman — two young entrepreneurs and immigration professionals with a shared mission: to bring integrity, transparency, and professionalism to the immigration services industry.

Frustrated by the lack of genuine support and the pressure-driven sales tactics common in the industry, we decided it was time for a change. Borderly was created to offer a better alternative — one built on trust, expertise, and a seamless client experience.

The Founders' Journey

Anshif began his journey by helping friends with student visas, which grew into a successful freelance practice.

Simon, a law school graduate, gained valuable insights at a leading immigration law firm, witnessing the systemic issues clients face.

Together, we combined our knowledge and experience to launch Borderly — a modern platform for honest guidance and stress-free documentation.
